Onsite Management intends to solve a big issue for construction companies who perform contractor duties. The eventual purpose of OnSite is to have an app that puts a time clock, a GPS tracker, a Certification verification all in one. With Onsite your employee’s will be able to clocking and you will know when they get to the job and when they leave. This performs another safety advantage because if an employee doesn’t leave the site you will have an approximate location of where the employee is.
